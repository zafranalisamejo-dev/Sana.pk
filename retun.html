Returns Policy

Hassle-free 2-day return policy for your peace of mind

Our 2-Day Return Policy

Simple, transparent, and customer-friendly

2 Days Return Window

You have 2 days from the date of delivery to request a return for eligible items.

Full Refund Guaranteed

Get a 100% refund on the product price for items returned in original condition within the 2-day window.

Free Replacement

If your item arrives damaged or defective, we will replace it at no extra cost — just contact us within 2 days.

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, please ensure the following

Item must be unused

Products must be in the same condition as received — unworn, unused, with no signs of use or damage caused by the customer.

Original packaging

Items must be returned in their original packaging, including all tags, accessories, and documentation.

Proof of purchase

Please include your order ID or receipt when requesting a return. This helps us process your request faster.

Contact within 2 days

Return requests must be initiated within 2 calendar days of the delivery date. Late requests may not be accepted.

Non-returnable items

Personal care items, intimate apparel, face masks, and perishable goods cannot be returned due to hygiene and safety reasons.

Custom or personalized orders

Custom-made or personalized products are not eligible for return unless they arrive damaged or defective.

How to Return an Item

Follow these simple steps to initiate a return

1

Contact Us

Call or WhatsApp us at 03449254538 within 2 days of delivery. Provide your order ID and reason for return.

2

Get Approval

Our team will verify your request and provide a Return Authorization (RA) number. Keep this for reference.

3

Pack the Item

Securely pack the item in its original packaging with all accessories. Include the order slip or a note with your RA number.

4

Ship the Item Back

Our courier partner will arrange a pickup from your address at no cost to you. Alternatively, you can drop it off at a designated location.

5

Refund or Replacement

Once we receive and inspect the item, we'll process your refund (2-3 business days) or ship a replacement immediately.

Damaged or Defective Items

We're here to make it right

Item Arrived Damaged?

If your order arrives damaged, please take a photo of the damaged item and packaging, and contact us immediately at 03449254538 within 2 days.

Defective Product?

If your product stops working or has a manufacturing defect, report it within 2 days. We'll arrange a free replacement with no additional shipping charges.

Wrong Item Received?

If you received a different item than what you ordered, let us know within 2 days and we'll ship the correct product immediately at no extra cost.

Need Help With a Return?

Our team is ready to assist you with any return-related queries.
