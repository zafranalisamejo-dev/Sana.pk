Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about shipping

Do you offer Cash on Delivery (COD)?

Yes! We offer Cash on Delivery (COD) across all cities in Pakistan. You can pay in cash when your order arrives at your doorstep. No advance payment is required for COD orders.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking ID via SMS and email. You can use this ID on our courier partner's website to track your package in real-time. You can also contact us for tracking updates.

What if my order arrives damaged?

If your order arrives damaged or defective, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery. We'll arrange a free replacement or full refund. Please keep the damaged item and packaging for inspection if requested.

Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?

Yes, you can request an address change within 2 hours of placing your order. After that, the order may already be in processing. Contact us immediately at 03449254538 and we'll do our best to accommodate the change.

Do you ship on weekends and public holidays?

Our courier partners operate Monday through Saturday. Orders placed on Friday after 3:00 PM, Saturday after 2:00 PM, or on public holidays will be processed the next business day. Delivery may be delayed on public holidays.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), Bank Transfer, Easypaisa, and JazzCash. For COD, payment is collected at the time of delivery. For bank transfers and mobile wallets, payment is confirmed before dispatch.
